LEVEL 7

The Encoded Message

Not every hidden message needs complex tools. Sometimes the disguise is so common that most people overlook it completely.

This level contains a piece of text that has been transformed using Base64. One way to notice it is the presence of = or == at the end of the string.

Decode the string below and submit the result as your flag.

RkxBR3tCYXNlNjRfSXNfRWFzeX0==

Base64 is not encryption. It is just encoding. Reverse it.

Most Base64 decoders work instantly. The output starts with FLAG{...}
