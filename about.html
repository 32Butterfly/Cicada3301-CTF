About This Project

Welcome! This is a web-based Capture The Flag (CTF) challenge inspired by the mysterious and legendary Cicada 3301 puzzles. These puzzles appeared online in 2012 and captured the imagination of cryptography enthusiasts, hackers, and curious minds around the world.

The original Cicada challenges combined cryptography, steganography, logic, and coding. Participants had to analyze images, decode hidden messages, and even explore obscure corners of the internet to find answers.

This CTF brings that same spirit here in your browser. You’ll progress through 10 levels, each introducing different techniques like Caesar ciphers, XOR encoding, Base64 decoding, and some sneaky client-side logic.

The focus is not just solving puzzles, but learning how data can be hidden, transformed, and revealed. It’s a playful way to train your mind in problem-solving and cryptography basics.
