LEVEL 8

The Console Cipher

This challenge hides its clues where most players never look. The numbers you need are not in the HTML, not in the image, and not written anywhere visible. You will have to inspect the browser console to find them.

Once you discover the sequence of numbers, your task is simple: decode them using a single operation. Every number must be XORed with 66 to reveal the final message.

Open the developer tools and check the console. The puzzle starts there.

XOR each number with 66. The result forms ASCII letters.
