Cicada’s Signature

You may be wondering why we need OutGuess, and how we can even know if the messages are made by the same organization or group of people. Well, that’s where the GPG key comes in. Cicada really likes to hide them inside images using OutGuess.

You know the drill by now: new image means you need to look through OutGuess for clues. Once you have the key ID, import it with GPG. The identity should be:

public key "NAME OF KEY" imported

You should give the answer: FLAG{NAME OF KEY} that is given after importing the key.

Extract the hidden data from the image. You are looking for a key ID.

Once you have the key ID, import it with GPG using the gpg --recv-keys command.
